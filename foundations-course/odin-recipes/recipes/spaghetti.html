Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that is easy to make and tastes delicious. It consists of spaghetti pasta topped with a rich and flavorful meat sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs. This hearty and satisfying dish is perfect for a family dinner or a casual meal with friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb spaghetti pasta

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain off any excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the beef. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onion is soft.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens and the flavors meld together.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti pasta according to the package instructions. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  6. Once the sauce is ready, serve it over the cooked spaghetti pasta. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
