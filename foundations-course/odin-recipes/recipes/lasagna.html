Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is perfect for feeding a crowd. It consists of layers of lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and a rich meat sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs. This hearty and comforting dish is perfect for a family dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain off any excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the beef. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onion is soft.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens and the flavors meld together.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain the noodles and set aside.
  6. In a large baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of ricotta cheese, a layer of mozzarella cheese, and a layer of Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
